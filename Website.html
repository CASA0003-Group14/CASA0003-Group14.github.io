Shrinking City----Detroit

Introduction

The aim of our project is to use data visualisation techniques to analyse the reasons why Detroit's city has shrunk dramatically in size. Thanks to the booming automotive industry, Detroit was once the fourth largest city in the country. But since the 1970s, the US has no longer been dominant and the Japanese and European automotive industries have recovered from the devastation of the Second World War. We will look at the national distribution of shrinking cities in the United States, The relationship between population and economy in Detroit, Urban Transformation in Detroit, Detroit's Future Urban Development Path, etc
